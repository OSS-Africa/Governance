# OSSAfrica Community Roadmap

**Building a secure, collaborative open source ecosystem across Africa through community-driven governance, education, and sustainable growth.**

This roadmap outlines OSSAfrica's strategic priorities and milestones for establishing foundational governance, launching country chapters, and delivering security-focused initiatives across the African continent.

## Scope

### What This Roadmap Covers
- Community building and chapter establishment
- Governance framework implementation
- Security education and tooling initiatives
- Event planning and execution
- Partnership development
- Infrastructure and operational systems

### What This Roadmap Does NOT Cover
- Commercial product development
- Individual company partnerships outside governance context
- Non-security related technical projects
- Regional political initiatives

## Strategic Objectives

1. **Establish foundational governance** - Implement transparent decision-making processes and community guidelines
2. **Launch country chapters** - Enable local leadership and community growth across 5+ African countries
3. **Deliver security education programs** - Provide accessible training and mentorship opportunities
4. **Build sustainable infrastructure** - Create scalable systems for community operations and collaboration
5. **Foster contributor engagement** - Develop clear pathways for participation and leadership development
6. **Host inaugural community events** - Organize virtual and in-person gatherings to strengthen connections
